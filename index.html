CCMN-2021 is a common platform for candidates to apply for M.Sc./ M.Sc. (Tech.) admissions to NITs and CFTI , based on their JAM score of year 2021. This year 20 NITs, IIEST Shibpur, and SLIET Longowal are participating for the CCMN counselling process. For details, please refer the list of Participating Institutes.

There will be online document verification process. For this purpose, after seat allotment, the candidates will be required to upload the needful documents. For all guidelines, the prospective candidates are advised to carefully read the information brochure and various other documents given in this website.

CCMN-2021 is being organized by Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur. In case of any query email us at ccmn2021help@mnit.ac.in
